# agent-assure v0.5.0

v0.5.0 adds streaming process-assurance artifacts and hardens the assurance
surface for release review.

Highlights:

- adds `stream-event-record`, `stream-ingestion-diagnostics`, and `stream-run`
  persisted roots under the v0.5.0 frozen schema snapshot;
- adds `agent-assure stream ingest` and `agent-assure stream evaluate` for
  privacy-filtered event streams, including deterministic ordering,
  duplicate/conflict handling, usage aggregation, and span-plan previews;
- projects streaming evidence into ordinary RunSets so existing expectation,
  evidence-link, human-review, provider/tool, and redaction controls continue
  to apply;
- enforces declared `required_policy_ids`, verdict-bearing persisted policy
  failures, complete ordinary run sets, and excluded-observation failures in
  deterministic evaluation;
- requires material-claim links to target content-addressed evidence items and
  requires expected human review to be both routed and observed as performed;
- keeps RAG, framework-adapter, live-statistics, usage, packet, and
  control-coverage evidence within the existing claim boundary.

Limitations:

- Streaming artifacts are review evidence only. They do not prove trace
  completeness, model safety, provider quality, compliance, clinical validity,
  or business impact.
- Counterfactual RAG fixtures remain authored synthetic evidence. They do not
  prove semantic equivalence or general retrieval robustness.
- Release evidence remains local reproducibility and review evidence, not a
  certification, attestation, endorsement, or legal conclusion.
